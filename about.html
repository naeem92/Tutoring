YOUR STUDENT IS UNIQUE

SO IS OUR APPROACH

With years of experience under our belts, the Ivy Tutoring administrative team is well-versed in the ins and outs of the local Folsom and El Dorado Hills school districts. We’re able to understand the types of courses, exams, personnel and learning tracks with which students interact on a daily basis. This level of knowledge and deep relationship with the community means that our entire leadership and entire team care deeply about the outcomes of students, as many of our personnel were once local students ourselves.

Our Services

Subject Tutoring

We offer tutoring in all academic levels. Get paired with a tutor to get help preparing for exams, reviewing homework, or just strengthening your concepts!

Collage Planning

Work alongside college counselors and successful university students to help plan for the journey ahead and maximum your changes of getting into your dream schools.

Test Prep

We will help you achieve your target scores in the SAT, ACT, and/or PSAT through practice exams and comprehensive review!
